A Passion for Junior hockey

The Parkwoods Midget Senior Stars was established in 2009 and plays at the Tier 1 Select level in the North York Hockey League (NYHL) located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. The majority of players have been on this team ever since they started playing youth hockey. The team has made four NYHL Tier 1 Finals appearances, winning the championship in 2013. Parkwoods Hockey League encourages more ice time, hence this select team only has thirteen skaters and two goalies. The head coach (right first) is Francis Cooke and the assistant coaches are James Cooke (left first) and Paul Magrath (left second).
