About Jesse Li

I am a grade 12 student going into my first-year at the University of Waterloo in Waterloo, Ontario for Computer Science. Growing up, I have always had a passion for hockey and I am happy to be a part of Canadian culture. I have been playing at Parkwoods Hockey League for eleven years, and have been on the select team for eight years.

At school, I am the head of tech crew club in charge of the audiovisual aspects of performances. My job is to train newer members how to use the equipment and help them settle into the new environment, assign duties and delegate roles, and communicate with other parties to ensure a smooth and enjoyable event.

Outside of school, my interest in mathematics inspired me to become a teaching assistant at Spirit of Math, where I assist younger students with their math work while promoting a positive and encouraging learning environment.

Giving back to the community is something I believe everybody should experience. For me, I thought it would be a great idea to share the hockey stories and skills I have gathered along the way by becoming a house league hockey coach. This opportunity allowed me to give the players the best experience on and off the ice, just like what other coaches did for me.

I have created this website using the Python programming language and Django web framework to showcase my hockey experience. Here, you will be able to see our team's short video clips, standings/schedules and various trophy winnigns. If you are registered, you can also create a topic and share your thoughts with others.

2020 marks the end of my junior hockey and high school journey. I'm looking forward to a new chapter of my life in 2020 and beyond.
